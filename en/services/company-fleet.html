CMR cargo insurance

All our cargo is insured with CMR insurance. With our CMR insurance, you will have peace of mind, because in the event of damage, you will be entitled to compensation for possible loss or damage to the goods during transport. Our insurance policy is adapted to the specific needs of transporters and provides a high level of coverage, which includes liability for the means of transport, goods and possible damage to third parties.

Vehicle specifications
